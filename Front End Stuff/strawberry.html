Home

Strawberry started in 2015, addressing the problems international students face when they want to study abroad and apply to American universities. Aiming to remove the barriers between international students and universities in United States, Strawberry provides resources about school information, essay writing, exam preparation, resume planning and current students matching to ease the application process
